about us

The College has a sprawling campus of over 12.94 acres. Since its inception, the college has been striving for excellence in the technical education by imparting effective teaching-learning process: To synthesize the needs of society and global market, to evolve urban and rural youth with the emphasis on rural area, under the able guidance of Sri V. Raghavendra Rao, B. Sc., Chairman, Sri V. Ramakrishna Rao, M. Com., Secretary and Sri S. Lakshmana Rao, B.A., B. L., Correspondent, who are Educationalists, Industrialists and Philanthropists. They are striving to make the Vision, Mission and Goals of the institution into reality.

Dr.P.Bala Krishna Prasad is the Principal of esteemed Eluru College of Engineering & Technology, ELURU. He received B.Tech (CSE) from IETE: New Delhi and M.Tech(CSE) from Andhra University in the year 2000. He obtained Ph.D in Computer Science & Engineering from Acharya Nagarjuna University GUNTUR in the year 2007.He has a rich experience of 21 years which includes Teaching, Research and Administration. He is a multi-tasking personality and served various designations ranging from Associate professor, Professor, Academic director and Principal for various reputed Engineering Colleges.

“Success comes to those, who work hard. And it stays with those, who don’t rest on the laurels of the past”.
